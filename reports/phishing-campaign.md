# Phishing Campaign Analysis

## Summary

Recent phishing activity appears to target financial services organizations through credential-harvesting emails and fake login portals.

## Indicators

- Suspicious domain: secure-update-login.com
- Example IP address: 185.203.116.42

## Observed Technique

Attackers attempt to imitate trusted organizations and redirect users to fake authentication pages.

## Potential Impact

- credential compromise
- unauthorized access
- financial fraud
- reputational damage

## Recommended Mitigation

- enable multi-factor authentication
- monitor suspicious login activity
- block known malicious domains
- increase phishing awareness training
